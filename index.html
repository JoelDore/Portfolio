My Story

- Accepted to UNH, but have no clue what to study... - After a year of consideration, I declared a major in Mathematics, where my love of problem-solving could be harnessed and developed into hard skills. - Of all the career paths in Maths that I knew of, none seem like the right fit- so I am left with a part-time maintenance job at Hannaford Supermarket... - Within a few years, I climbed through various supervisory roles, and ultimately accepted a position as Evening Operations Manager, helping lead multiple stores throughout NH/ME. - I have developed invaluable leadership and time-management skills, but my longing for creativity and technical problem-solving is strong... - My incredibly supportive wife took a travelling job in the medical field, giving me the flexibility to step down , and enroll in UNH's Coding Boot Camp where my love for creativity and problem-solving has been growing ever since! - Apparently I can't keep coding 24/7... - In my free time, I love to do puzzles, play music and explore the beauty of nature with my wonderful wife!
