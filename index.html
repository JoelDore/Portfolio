About Me

Joel Dore is originally from Dover, NH but is currently traveling the U.S. with his wife and cat. After 6 years in retail/management, he is now pursuing a career in web development.

A mathematician at heart, Joel is fascinated by solving complex problems and is naturally obsessed with patterns, puzzles, logic and animation. When he is not coding, Joel loves playing music and exploring the beauty of nature. Harnessing his strengths and passions and developing them in ways that help people is something Joel continues to prioritize both in life and in his work.
